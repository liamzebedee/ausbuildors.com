About

Helping each other out, connecting crypto larrikins

Aus Buildooors is Australia's grassroots crypto builder community. 460+ members strong across one group chat, with monthly meetups in Sydney (since November 2022) and Brisbane.

We're devs, founders, researchers, degens, and creatives building onchain. No suits, no pitch decks, no networking cringe — just builders sharing what they're working on over beers.

Purpose

Why we exist

Local connections

Crypto can be isolating. We bring together Aussie builders who are often working remotely on global protocols, giving them a local community.

Knowledge sharing

Monthly talks and demos from members working across DeFi, infrastructure, NFTs, ZK, and everything else onchain.

Incubation

Some of the best ideas start as conversations at our meetups. We've seen collaborations, co-founders, and new projects emerge from the community.
